1. ANNUAL LEAVE ENTITLEMENT
----------------------------------------------------------------------
All permanent full-time employees are entitled to 25 days of paid annual
leave per calendar year. This entitlement increases progressively based on
years of service:

  - 0–2 years of service:   25 days per year
  - 3–5 years of service:   27 days per year
  - 6–10 years of service:  30 days per year
  - 10+ years of service:   33 days per year

Part-time employees receive a pro-rated annual leave entitlement based on
their contracted hours relative to a full-time equivalent (FTE). Leave
accrues at a rate of 2.08 days per month for full-time employees.

New joiners who start employment after January 1 will receive a prorated
leave allocation based on the number of full months remaining in the
calendar year.

2. ANNUAL LEAVE APPLICATION PROCESS
----------------------------------------------------------------------
Employees must apply for annual leave at least TWO (2) WEEKS in advance
for leave of 3 or more consecutive days. For single-day or two-day leave,
a minimum of 48 hours' notice is required.

All leave requests must be submitted via the HR Self-Service Portal at
https://hr.techcorp.internal/leave. The following steps outline the process:

  Step 1: Log in to the HR Portal using your employee credentials.
  Step 2: Navigate to "My Leave" → "Request New Leave".
  Step 3: Select leave type "Annual Leave" from the dropdown menu.
  Step 4: Enter the start date, end date, and any coverage arrangements.
  Step 5: Provide a brief reason (optional for annual leave).
  Step 6: Click "Submit Request" — your direct manager will receive an
          automated email notification for approval.
  Step 7: You will receive an email confirmation once your manager
          approves or rejects the request (typically within 2 business days).

Emergency leave exceptions (e.g., bereavement, sudden illness) may be
communicated verbally first and formalized in the HR Portal within 3
business days of return.

3. SICK LEAVE
----------------------------------------------------------------------
Employees are entitled to 10 days of paid sick leave per calendar year.
Sick leave does not accrue or carry over to the following year.

  - For absences of 1–3 consecutive days: Self-certification is acceptable.
    Employees must notify their line manager by 9:00 AM on the first day
    of absence via phone or the HR Portal messaging system.

  - For absences exceeding 3 consecutive days: A valid medical certificate
    signed by a registered physician is MANDATORY. The certificate must be
    submitted to HR within 5 business days of return.

  - Chronic illness management: Employees with recurring medical conditions
    should contact HR Wellbeing Services to arrange a confidential meeting
    and explore reasonable workplace accommodations.

Unauthorized absence (absence not reported or not supported by valid
documentation) may result in unpaid deduction and disciplinary action
under the company's Conduct Policy.

4. MATERNITY LEAVE
----------------------------------------------------------------------
Eligible employees who are primary caregivers following the birth or
adoption of a child are entitled to 26 weeks (182 days) of maternity leave,
structured as follows:

  - Weeks 1–16 (Compulsory Period): Paid at 100% of base salary.
  - Weeks 17–26 (Optional Extended Period): Paid at 50% of base salary.

To qualify, employees must have completed a minimum of 12 months of
continuous service with TechCorp before the expected due date. Employees
must inform HR at least 12 weeks before the expected start of maternity
leave by submitting a "Maternity Leave Notification Form" available on the
HR Portal. A copy of the medical certificate confirming expected due date
is required.

Employees on maternity leave continue to accrue annual leave during
the compulsory paid period (Weeks 1–16). Pension contributions continue
at the normal rate during the fully-paid period.

5. PATERNITY LEAVE
----------------------------------------------------------------------
Secondary caregivers (typically fathers or non-birthing partners) are
entitled to 4 weeks (28 days) of fully paid paternity leave. This leave
must be taken within 56 days of the child's birth or placement.

Paternity leave notification must be given at least 6 weeks in advance via
the HR Portal. Employees must provide the expected due date or adoption
placement date at the time of application.

In cases of multiple births (twins, triplets, etc.) or adoption, paternity
leave entitlement remains 4 weeks but can be split into two separate periods
within the 56-day window.

6. SHARED PARENTAL LEAVE
----------------------------------------------------------------------
Parents may choose to share up to 37 weeks of paid leave between both
partners after the compulsory 2-week maternity period. Shared parental
leave can be taken in blocks and must be agreed upon with HR at least
8 weeks in advance. Both parents must be employed at TechCorp to
utilize shared parental leave simultaneously.

7. CARRY-OVER RULES
----------------------------------------------------------------------
Employees are permitted to carry over a MAXIMUM of 5 days of unused annual
leave into the following calendar year. Carry-over days must be used by
March 31 of the subsequent year or they will be forfeited without
compensation.

Exceptions to the 5-day carry-over limit may be granted in the following
circumstances:
  - Long-term sick leave: Employees who could not take leave due to medical
    absence may carry over their full unused entitlement.
  - Operational requirements: Where the company has required an employee to
    postpone approved leave due to urgent business needs. This exception
    requires written confirmation from the HR Director.

Any carry-over days granted under exception must be taken within 18 months
of the original leave year end.

8. PUBLIC HOLIDAYS
----------------------------------------------------------------------
TechCorp observes 12 statutory public holidays per calendar year. Employees
are entitled to a paid day off on each of these days. If a public holiday
falls on a weekend, the next available working day is observed as the
substitute bank holiday.

2024 Public Holidays (England & Wales):
  1.  January 1        — New Year's Day
  2.  March 29         — Good Friday
  3.  April 1          — Easter Monday
  4.  May 6            — Early May Bank Holiday
  5.  May 27           — Spring Bank Holiday
  6.  August 26        — Summer Bank Holiday
  7.  October 31       — Company Foundation Day (TechCorp exclusive)
  8.  December 25      — Christmas Day
  9.  December 26      — Boxing Day
  10. December 27      — Christmas Substitute Day
  11. December 30      — New Year's Eve Observed
  12. December 31      — Year-End Closure Day

Employees required to work on a public holiday due to business-critical
operations will receive time-off-in-lieu (TOIL) at a 1.5x rate, to be
taken within 90 days.

9. LEAVE WITHOUT PAY (LWOP)
----------------------------------------------------------------------
Employees who have exhausted their annual leave entitlement may apply for
Leave Without Pay for personal reasons. LWOP is granted entirely at the
discretion of the employee's department head and the HR Director. Maximum
LWOP duration in a calendar year is 10 days. LWOP does not affect
continuity of service but pension contributions are paused during this period.

10. BEREAVEMENT LEAVE
----------------------------------------------------------------------
Employees are granted 5 days of paid bereavement leave for the death of an
immediate family member (spouse, child, parent, sibling). Two days of paid
leave are provided for extended family (grandparent, in-laws, aunt, uncle).
Bereavement leave is separate from annual leave and does not affect the
annual leave balance.

11. STUDY LEAVE
----------------------------------------------------------------------
Full-time employees enrolled in company-approved training or professional
certification programs may apply for up to 10 days of paid study leave per
year. Study leave applications must be submitted with proof of enrollment
and the examination/coursework schedule at least 4 weeks in advance.
